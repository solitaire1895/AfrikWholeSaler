========================================================
AFRIKWHOLESALER — VISUAL IDENTITY & DESIGN SYSTEM
========================================================

MISSION

Create a premium, modern, enterprise-grade visual identity for AfrikWholesaler, an AI-powered sourcing and wholesale platform that connects African businesses with products manufactured in China.

The design language must communicate:

• Trust
• Professionalism
• Reliability
• Global Commerce
• Innovation
• Premium Service
• Simplicity
• Speed

The overall experience should feel comparable to Stripe, Apple, Linear, Notion, Shopify, and Amazon Business.
